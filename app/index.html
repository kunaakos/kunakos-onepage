PizzaPhone

It's a rotary phone converted to a speaking alarm clock. Tells you the time and current settings if you pick it up and you can use the dial for input. It was made to be as inefficent as possible by today's standards.

It has a wonderfully annoying voice, courtesy of hyperdunno.

  • Arduino
  • AVR C++
